Easy Roasted Broccoli

This easy roasted broccoli is easy to make and will satisfy even the pickiest of eaters.

You'll find a detailed ingredients list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients:

  • 14 ounces broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cut broccoli florets from the stalk. Peel the stalk and slice into 1/4-inch slices. Mix florets and stem pieces with olive oil in a bowl and trasfer to a baking sheet; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until broccoli is tender and lightly browned, about 18 to 20 minutes.

Nutrition Facts:

  • 63 Calories
  • 4g Fat
  • 7g Carbs
  • 3g Protein
